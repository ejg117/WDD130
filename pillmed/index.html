About Drugs

Drugs have been a part of human culture for centuries, used for medicinal, recreational, and spiritual purposes. They can be classified into various categories based on their effects on the body and mind, such as stimulants, depressants, hallucinogens, and opioids.

What to Know

Understanding the basics about drugs is essential for making informed decisions about their use. This includes knowing the potential benefits and risks associated with different types of drugs, as well as being aware of the legal and health implications of drug use.

Drugs can be powerful tools for healing, but they can also cause harm if misused. Education and awareness are key to ensuring that drugs are used safely and responsibly.
